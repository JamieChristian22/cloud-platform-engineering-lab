# CloudWatch Dashboards

## Purpose

CloudWatch dashboards provide a centralized view of platform health and operational performance.

## Dashboard Name

`Enterprise-Platform-Operations`

## Recommended Widgets

- EC2 CPU utilization
- EC2 NetworkIn
- EC2 NetworkOut
- EC2 StatusCheckFailed
- Alarm state summary
- Log group activity
- Platform KPI notes

## Enterprise Value

Dashboards help platform engineers quickly determine whether systems are healthy. They reduce troubleshooting time and support proactive operations.
