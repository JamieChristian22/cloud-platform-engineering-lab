# Troubleshooting Guide

## Cannot Create Organization
Check permissions and billing/account status.

## Cannot Create OU
Confirm you are in the management account and selected Root.

## SCPs Not Available
Confirm all features are enabled.

## Cannot Attach SCP
Confirm target OU exists and your permissions allow policy attachment.

## Policy Too Restrictive
Detach from OU, review JSON, and test in Sandbox before reattaching.
