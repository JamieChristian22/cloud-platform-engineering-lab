# Governance Architecture Decisions

## AWS Organizations
Used for centralized cloud account governance.

## Organizational Units
Created Infrastructure, Security, Workloads, and Sandbox OUs to separate responsibility areas.

## Service Control Policies
Used to demonstrate enterprise guardrails and maximum permission boundaries.

## Sandbox Policy Testing
The Sandbox OU is the safest place to test governance controls before applying them broadly.
